Mobile nav

Today the marketing pages hide every product link below 800px (only the wordmark survives) and docs squeezes a scrolling strip of six links between a floating hamburger and the API-key button. Three directions for (www), one aligned treatment for docs. All variants keep the 48px bar, hairlines-not-shadows, and the mono-uppercase label voice.
