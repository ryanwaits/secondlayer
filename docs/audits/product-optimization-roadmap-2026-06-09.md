# Secondlayer Product Roadmap — Synthesis

## Thesis
Secondlayer becomes the agent-native data plane for Stacks: three layers (Streams raw → Index decoded → Subgraphs custom), one free Catalog, webhooks as delivery mode — every layer pull/push/bulk, queryable by humans, payable by agents. The wedge is Index (keyless curl, agent-discoverable, x402-payable); Subgraphs is the priced upsell; Streams retains whales. Revenue comes from selling limits on shared infra to ~15-25 named Stacks teams via founder-led sales — not from funnels, meters, or micro-payments.

## Surface verdicts
- **Index** — KEEP, crown as THE wedge (overrule VISION.md). Target: every Stacks app dev + accountless agents. Keyless curl on homepage.
- **Subgraphs** — KEEP as monetization core. Target: devs with real data models (marketplaces, DeFi dashboards). Unblock: reads currently locked behind control-plane auth.
- **Streams** — KEEP as raw layer + whale retention. Target: infra/indexer builders (stacking.club persona).
- **Subscriptions** — DEMOTE to "Webhooks," delivery feature of Index+Subgraphs. Positioning-only: nav/docs rename, API/SDK identifiers untouched. Target: bot/ops builders.
- **Datasets** — DEMOTE to free public Catalog (keep name — it's the grant artifact; keep `/v1/datasets` + SDK as thin resolvers, zero deletions). Target: researchers, Foundation, demand-gen.

## Roadmap

### Now (0-30d)
1. **Minimum-billable billing** — packaging surgery: plans = rate-tier limits (delete container vocab + launch/build drift), 3 flat tiers, /billing = plan card + Stripe Checkout link, hard caps + 429-with-upgrade-CTA. No metered billing. [pricing] **M**
2. **Founder-led sales motion** — name 25 target accounts (wallets, sBTC protocols, stacking services, explorers); pitch 10 design-partner deals $500-1.5k/mo w/ white-glove subgraph setup. The only path to $20k. [GTM] **M, ongoing**
3. **Subgraph reads → `/v1` open-read** — standard cursor envelope, index-style anon limits; NO x402 bundled. Pair with 2-3 deployed showcase subgraphs publicized in Discord/X — the pairing is the growth mechanic. [streamline|agent-native] **M**
4. **x402 marketing launch (timeboxed 3 days)** — publish 14 changesets, fund sponsor key small + hard cap + kill switch, fix reconciler decoder-lag guard + 2h-lookback strand, pin worker replicas=1 (no leader election), demo "first API an agent pays for on Bitcoin." Kill criterion: <$50 organic in 60d → freeze all x402 work. [GTM] **S**
5. **PgBouncer** — one container, transaction pooling, one day, both planes. First scaling wall, $0 fix. [tech] **S**
6. **Redis correctness** — fail-loud when 2+ replicas lack REDIS_URL; split `index:anon` bucket per-IP. x402 + tier-enforcement prereq. [tech|pricing] **S**
7. **60-sec scaffold→deploy→query hero demo** — video, pin everywhere, DM directly to the ~20 teams running custom indexers; offer to scaffold THEIR contract live. [GTM] **S**
8. **Positioning sweep (S chores batched)** — Webhooks nav rename, Catalog page, "Streams=raw / Index=decoded / Subgraphs=yours" framing everywhere, VISION/Chainhooks doc reconciliation, drop `ai` dep from `@secondlayer/stacks` + strong README. [streamline] **S**

### Next (30-90d)
1. **GROUP BY / time-bucket aggregates** — whitelisted (`date_trunc` + count/sum/avg/min/max), bounded time range, statement timeout, row cap. First read-side paid gate: free = 7-day windows, full history = paid. [agent-native|pricing] **M**

3. **"Kill your Chainhook" migration guide** — predicate→subscription mappings; enumerate Chainhook users (GitHub search, Hiro Discord), send directly; CTA → paid tier. [GTM] **S**
4. **Foundation grant application** — Catalog/Datasets as the deliverable. $150-300k runway, highest-EV single action; pursue in parallel, don't count as MRR. [GTM] **M**
5. **x402 in MCP** — only if kill criterion passed; small lift since SDK client exists. [agent-native] **S**
6. **OpenAPI S-fix** — hand-add missing endpoints (contracts/SSE/usage/dumps) + CI drift test asserting every mounted `/v1` route appears in spec. Chore, not a bet. [tech] **S**
7. **WAL archiving on postgres-platform** — billing/x402 ledger must-never-lose; cost ≈ nothing. [tech] **S**
8. **Handler safety S-substitute** — execution timeout + crash-restart supervision + one-page security answer ("gated deploys + review, roadmap to isolation"). For sales conversations only. [tech] **S**

### Later (90d+)
1. **Stripe metered billing** — gate: ≥10 paying accounts or first cap-exceeder. Until then, invoice overage manually from SQL. [pricing] **M**
2. **`sl subgraphs dev`** — downscoped: hot-reload replaying hosted Streams dumps into local Postgres, no node/devnet. Gate: evidence of deploy-to-test churn; deferrable for any named-account deal. [streamline] **M**
3. **Tip-follower deep-gap recovery via own node RPC** — removes Hiro from live path entirely. [tech] **M**
4. **Dataset-publisher framework** — kill the 7-clone pattern when Catalog growth demands it. [tech] **M**
5. **x402 graduation bridge** — concierge first ("email your payer principal, we mint a key"); automate only after repeat organic payers. [pricing] **S→M**

7. **Multichain desk memo** — 2-3 day decoder-portability research note only; go/no-go gated on Stacks ≥$3-5k MRR. [pricing] **S**
8. **Idempotency-Key on `/api` mutations + coded errors** — when agent automation traffic is real. [agent-native] **M**

## Pricing plan
**Sell limits on shared infra (~99% margin), not dormant provisioning.**

| Limit | Free | Pro $99 | Scale $499 | Ent $1.5k+ |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Index/Streams reads | 10 rps | 50 rps | 250 rps | custom |
| Subgraphs | 2 | 10 | 50 + BYO DB | unlimited + custom decoders |
| Webhook deliveries | 10k/mo | 250k | 2M | SLA |
| SSE connections / backfills | 2 / 1 | 10 / 3 | 50 / 10 | dedicated |
| Aggregate history | 7 days | full | full | full |

Free forever: Catalog/datasets, parquet dumps, discovery JSON, contract discovery. Overage: manual invoices until ≥10 paying accounts. Enforcement: hard caps + upgrade CTA, soft-cap freeze as the anti-surprise-bill differentiator.

**x402 role:** acquisition wedge + category-creating PR ("agents pay per call, no signup"), NOT a revenue line — ~1M paid calls/mo per $1k MRR. Route weights above gas floor (reads $0.002, aggregates $0.01, backfill $0.05); accept dust; value is narrative + funnel.

**Honest MRR math:** 12mo realistic = 15 Pro ($1.5k) + 6 Scale ($3k) + 2-3 design-partner/Ent ($3-4.5k) + overage/x402 dust ≈ **$8-10k MRR**. $20k from Stacks self-serve alone is not reachable. The gap closes via: (a) one anchor partner deal at $5-8k/mo (PRODUCTS.md's "Partner Platform is the ceiling" — Hiro-alternative positioning to a wallet/explorer-scale buyer), plus (b) Foundation grant as runway (not MRR), plus (c) gated multichain option only after (a) is attempted. Plan and communicate internally on $8-10k; treat $20k as anchor-deal-dependent.
